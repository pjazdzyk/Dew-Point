HVAC-LIB

PHYSICS OF AIR WITH WATER VAPOUR

Author: Piotr Jażdżyk
Harvard University CS50x Final Project

FUNCTIONALITY

    HUMID AIR PROPERTIES
  • water vapour saturation pressure
  • dew point temperature
  • wet bulb temperature
  • air relative humidity
  • humidity ratio and maximum humidity ratio
  • kinematic and dynamic viscosity
  • thermal conductivity
  • specific enthalpy with water and ice mist
  • specific heat
  • density
  • thermal diffusivity
  • Prandtl number

    • HEATING AND COOLING
  • driven by heat input / output
  • driven by target outlet temperature
  • driven by target relative humidity

    LIQUID WATER PROPERTIES
  • specific heat
  • density
  • specific enthalpy

    • PHYSICS OF FLOW
  • mass flow
  • volumetric flow
  • mass flow of dry air component
  • volumetric flow of dry air component

    • AIR MIXING
  • simple mixing of two flows with humidity
  • mixing of multiple flows with humidity

    • IN DEVELOPMENT:
  • public REST API
  • unit conversion system
  • air humidification process
  • air heat recovery process
